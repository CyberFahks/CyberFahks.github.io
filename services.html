Hi! My name is Deanna (Dee-na) and I'm a professional graphic designer.

Welcome to my portfolio. I have a bachelor's degree in Graphic Design; with a background in cybersecurity and a transition to Graphic Design, I bring a unique blend of analytical thinking and creative flair to my work. My journey has been guided by a commitment to delivering excellence, so I specialize in creating visuals that resonate with my clients. Whether it's conceptualizing brand identities, crafting engaging illustrations, or refining digital artworks, I thrive on turning imagination into impactful designs.
