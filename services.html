My Graphic Design Workflow

Hello, and welcome to my graphic design portfolio! My name is Deanna and I am a freelance graphic designer. The technical boring stuff is below if you are interested in my workflow.

  • 1. Discovery & Research

    I begin by understanding your needs and goals. This involves researching your brand, target audience, and industry trends to ensure the design aligns with your vision and objectives.

  • 2. Concept Development

    INext, I brainstorm and sketch initial ideas. This phase includes creating mood boards and rough drafts to explore various concepts and approaches.

  • 3. Design & Refinement

    With a clear concept in mind, I start crafting the design using industry-standard tools. This includes selecting colors, typography, and imagery, and refining the details to ensure everything is cohesive and visually appealing.

  • 4. Feedback & Revisions

    I present the design to you for feedback. Based on your input, I make necessary revisions to ensure the final piece meets your expectations and effectively communicates your message.

  • 5. Finalization & Delivery

    Once the design is approved, I prepare the final files in the required formats and resolutions. I deliver the completed design, ready for print or digital use, and provide any additional support you may need.
